What does Rotaract do?

Rotaract originally began as a Rotary International youth program in 1968 by 'Paul Harris'. "Rotaract" stands for "Rotary in Action", although the name originally comes from a combination of "Rotary" and "Interact" (International + Action), the high school level program created by Rotary International.The motive of Rotaract is: Self-development through fellowship and service for young people from 16 to 30 years. It is an organization which helps Individuals to grow & network through it's projects & fellowships Internationally.
